About the Game

Welcome to Busy Worker Run, the ultimate test of reflexes for the modern professional. In this fast-paced infinite runner, you play as a diligent office worker trying to navigate the chaotic city streets to get to work on time. It's not just a run; it's a battle against the urban elements!

The Story

Every morning is a new challenge. The sidewalk is treacherous, filled with spilled Coffee that can ruin your shoes, hungry Pigeons taking flight at the worst moments, and reckless Electric Scooters zooming past on the wrong side of the road. Your goal is simple: Run as far as you can, dodge every obstacle, and achieve the highest score possible.

Game Features

  • Infinite Gameplay: The city never ends, and neither does the challenge. The longer you run, the faster it gets!
  • Dynamic Obstacles: Jump over puddles of coffee, dodge flying pigeons, and watch out for speeding scooters overtaking you.
  • Retro Pixel Art: Enjoy a nostalgic aesthetic with custom-designed pixel art characters and environments.
  • High Score Tracking: Challenge yourself to beat your personal best. Your high score is saved locally!
